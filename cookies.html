Cookies & small crumbs

We use cookies sparingly, like scattered seeds. This page explains what they are, which ones we set, and how you can manage them — always with your privacy in mind.

Our cookie philosophy

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. We use them to keep our site running smoothly, remember your preferences, and understand (anonymously) how our content is enjoyed. We never use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

Types of cookies we use

We group cookies into three categories. You can control them via your browser settings or our preference panel (where available).

Managing your cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, delete existing cookies, or set preferences per site.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

You can also opt out of our analytics cookie by not enabling it when prompted (or by clearing the analytics_opt cookie).

Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising, social media, or analytics. The only exception is our privacy-first newsletter provider, who uses a single functional cookie to manage subscription preferences — this is set only after you interact with the newsletter form.

Cookie questions

Do you use cookies for tracking?

No. We only use essential cookies for functionality. Our analytics cookie is optional and disabled unless you explicitly allow it.

How do I disable cookies?

You can disable cookies in your browser settings (see links above). Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Do you share cookie data with third parties?

No. Cookie data stays on your device and is not shared with any third party except the newsletter provider (who processes subscription requests).

How long do cookies last?

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Functional and preference cookies last up to 1 year. Analytics cookies last 30 days.
