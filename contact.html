Frequently asked questions

Do you offer guided forest walks?

Yes — we lead small-group mycology walks in the Affric region. Contact us for availability and seasonal schedules.

Can I send you a mushroom specimen for identification?

Absolutely. Please include location, habitat, and photos. We’ll do our best to help, but we can’t guarantee edibility advice.
