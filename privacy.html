Privacy & the forest

We honour your data as we honour the old growth. This policy describes what we collect, why, and how we protect it — with transparency and care.

🔒 data protection 🌱 minimal collection 📜 GDPR compliant

Our commitment

Briar Hollowcrest Moss respects your privacy. We collect only the information that helps us communicate with you, improve our offerings, and protect our community. We never sell or trade your personal data.

What we collect

  • Contact details — name, email address, and any message you send via our forms.
  • Newsletter subscriptions — email address and subscription preferences.
  • Usage data — anonymised analytics (page views, referrals) to understand how our forest of content is explored.
  • Cookies — functional cookies for site performance, and optional analytics cookies (see our Cookies Policy).

minimal by design We do not collect sensitive data unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., in a message).

How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiries and correspondence.
  • To send you seasonal field notes and updates (only if you opt in).
  • To improve our website and content based on aggregate usage.
  • To comply with legal obligations, if required.

We process data under the lawful basis of legitimate interest and consent (for newsletters).

Data storage & security

Your data is stored on secure servers within the UK/EU. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined, or as required by law.

  • Contact messages: retained for up to 3 years.
  • Newsletter subscribers: retained until you unsubscribe.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@briarhollowcrest.com.

Third-party links & services

Our site may include links to external websites (e.g., social media, RSS). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

We use minimal third-party services: our newsletter is managed via a privacy-first provider, and analytics are anonymised (no IP tracking).

🔐 0 data sales — ever 📬 48h typical response time for privacy requests

Privacy questions & answers

Do you use cookies for tracking?

We use only essential cookies for functionality. Optional analytics cookies are disabled by default — you can enable them via our cookies policy page.

How do I unsubscribe from the newsletter?

Every newsletter includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us directly and we’ll remove you within 48 hours.

Is my data shared with third parties?

No. We do not share, sell, or rent your data. We use a newsletter provider (who is also GDPR compliant) solely for sending emails.

How long do you keep my messages?

We retain contact form messages for up to 3 years to maintain correspondence history, unless you request deletion earlier.
