    Frequently asked questions

    Is foraging for wild mushrooms safe?

    Yes, with proper identification. We always recommend using multiple field guides and never consuming any fungus you cannot identify with 100% certainty.

    How do I start a mycology journal?

    Begin with a simple notebook, sketch the mushroom, note the habitat, weather, and substrate. Over time, you’ll build a personal reference.

    What is the best mushroom for a beginner to cultivate?

    Oyster mushrooms (Pleurotus) are forgiving and fast-growing — ideal for first-time growers.
